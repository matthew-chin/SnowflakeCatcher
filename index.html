COntrol the fate of solid water today!

Click and drag to draw a red line that stops the falling snowflakes

Press '0' to change the color of your line to green, which makes the snowflakes speed up

Click and drag with the right mouse button to erase your line.

Press 'r' to make the entire screen red and stop all the snowflakes. Press 'r' again to undo

Press 'g' to continuously make the screen green for super fast snowflakes. Press 'g' again to undo
